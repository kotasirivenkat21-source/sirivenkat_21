📊 Adani Enterprises — Stock Data Analysis (Power BI)
📝 Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing historical stock data for Adani Enterprises Limited, covering price movement, trading volume, and 52-week high/low trends across the year.
🛠️ Tools Used

📗 Excel — raw data source / cleaning
🧮 Power Query — data transformation
📊 Power BI — dashboard & visualization
🧠 DAX — measures (Sum/Avg calculations)

🔑 Key Metrics (Cards)

📈 Avg of 52W High: 3.91K
📉 Avg of 52W Low: 1.14K
💰 Close Total: 667.66K
💵 Open Total: 669.55K+
🔁 Volume Total: 876M

📈 Visuals Built

📅 Sum of No. of Trades by Month — line chart
📆 Daily Totals by Date — Avg Close vs Avg Open trend
🧱 Sum of VOLUME by Month — horizontal bar chart
📊 Monthly 52W High & Low + Volume — clustered column chart
🗓️ Sum of 52W H & 52W L by Day — day-wise trend line

🎛️ Interactivity

🔍 Filters: Date, Month, Year, Quarter
🔀 Drill-through & Cross-report filtering enabled
🖱️ Slicers for dynamic time-based analysis

📂 Fields Used
Date, Day, HIGH, LOW, close, OPEN, PREV. CLOSE, VOLUME, 52W H, 52W L, Itp, Month, Quarter, Year, No. of trades, series, vwap
✅ Insights Delivered

Identified peak trading months (May & August had highest volume)
Tracked daily open/close price volatility
Compared 52-week high vs low trends across the year
